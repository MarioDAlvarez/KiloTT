Features

Simple

With only a single cog and chainring, maintenance is simple. No shifters, derailleurs, or cassettes to have to deal with.

Durable

Made of 520 Reynolds double-butted chromoly steel. Light enough for daily use, yet strong enough for year-round of abuse.

Stylish

Sleek lines and sharp angles for an track-inspired look with a lugged fork for classic styling. Offered in various colors to suit all tastes.
